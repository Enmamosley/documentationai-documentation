## Overview

Use this guide to get oriented, complete a few first actions, and build confidence with the starter kit.

<Callout kind="info">
  This guide stays generic so it fits the starter kit. Replace each step with your product-specific setup as you adapt the page.
</Callout>

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="tip">
  Before you begin, have access to the starter kit, a place to edit content, and any sign-in details your setup requires.
</Callout>

## First steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the starter kit" icon="folder" titleType="p">
    Open the project or workspace you want to use and confirm that the default content loads.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the starting content" icon="book-open" titleType="p">
    Scan the existing pages, navigation, and sample copy so you know what to keep, what to replace, and where your own content belongs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make your first update" icon="edit" titleType="p">
    Edit one page with your own title, short description, and a small piece of real content. Save the change and check that it appears where you expect.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your setup" icon="check-circle" titleType="p">
    Reopen the page, verify that the layout still looks right, and note anything you want to refine before you add more content.
  </Step>
</Steps>
